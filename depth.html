What Depth is

Not every story fits in a headline.

The Nautisk was built on the belief that complex news should be understandable to everyone. Depth is where we take that belief the furthest. These are the pieces that require weeks of research, multiple sources, and the kind of careful writing that does justice to the full weight of a subject.

We cover the same terrain as The Nautisk — finance, law, healthcare, and politics — but here we go further. We follow the money across multiple quarters. We trace a law through every amendment. We map a healthcare system across every stakeholder. We give you the full picture.

Depth is for readers who are not satisfied with knowing what happened. They want to know why, how, and what it means for what comes next.

There are no artificial length limits here. A piece is as long as the story requires and no longer. Every word earns its place.

How we work

Research-first

Every Depth piece begins with primary sources — data, documents, filings, and records — before a single word is written.

No artificial limits

Pieces are as long as the story demands. Some will be 1,500 words. Others will run much longer. Length follows substance, not convention.

Full transparency

Every claim is sourced. Every methodology is explained. Readers should be able to follow our reasoning from first principle to final conclusion.
