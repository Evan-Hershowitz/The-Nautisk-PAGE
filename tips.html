Submit your tip

Fill out as much or as little as you are comfortable sharing. Name and email are entirely optional. Your message is the only required field.

A note on anonymity. You are never required to identify yourself. If you share your contact details, they will only be used to follow up on your tip — never published. If you request confidentiality, we will honor it. For highly sensitive tips, we recommend contacting us via Signal at the number listed on this page.

How tips work

Every submission goes directly to our newsroom and is reviewed personally. We read all tips even when we cannot respond to each one individually.

If your tip leads to a published story and you have requested anonymity, we will not identify you as a source — ever.

Tips may be used for reporting purposes across any section of The Nautisk including Depth investigations.

Need to reach us securely?

For sensitive tips involving confidential documents, whistleblower situations, or anything where your safety is a concern, we recommend using Signal for encrypted communication.
